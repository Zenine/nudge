# Code of Conduct

Nudge follows a concise Contributor Covenant-style standard.

## Our pledge

We aim to make participation in this project respectful, welcoming, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback and explain tradeoffs.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect privacy; do not ask contributors to share private calendars, health exports, secrets, or personal data.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, private exports, or sensitive local data.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or maintainer decisions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or block participants who violate this code. Report conduct concerns through GitHub issue or moderation features without posting private information.
